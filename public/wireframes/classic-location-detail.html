About This Location

Wilsons Promontory is Victoria's southernmost point, offering spectacular coastal scenery, pristine beaches, and diverse wildlife. Stock Yard is a popular camping area with excellent facilities for families.

Best visited during spring and autumn for mild weather. The area features numerous walking tracks, beach access, and abundant wildlife viewing opportunities.

Key Features

✓ Powered sites available
✓ Hot showers & toilets
✓ Beach access (2km)
✓ Bush walking tracks
✓ Wildlife viewing
✓ BBQ facilities
